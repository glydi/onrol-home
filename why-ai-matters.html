Why AI Matters Now

AI has moved from a research curiosity to the most leveraged skill of the decade. The people who learn to build with it — not just talk about it — are pulling ahead fast.

The gap is widening

Every industry is being rebuilt around AI tooling. Those who can turn a prompt into a working product, automate real workflows, and ship are becoming far more valuable than those who only consume content about AI.

You don't need to be an engineer

The barrier to building has collapsed. With vibe coding and modern AI tools, a motivated beginner can ship products that used to require a full team — if they know how to execute.

Timing is the advantage

We're early. The habits, portfolio, and reputation you build now compound. Waiting another year means competing against people who started today.
